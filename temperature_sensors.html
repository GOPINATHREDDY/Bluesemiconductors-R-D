TEMPERATURE SENSOR

Advancements in semiconductor technologies demand fully integrated temperature sensors that consume less area, low power and are built using an all CMOS process. Integrated temperature sensors are widely used in ICs that require compensation in output for the temperature variation and are used in conjunction with sensors like accelerometers, gyroscopes and magnetometers in mobile, robotics and healthcare applications
